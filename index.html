CLIMATE CHANGE AND WATER TREATMENT

Currently the world has experienced adverse changes in climate which have affected the availability of water this is to say that there is limited access to clean water sources, however in correspondence with the climatic changes, technology has made it possible to mitigate the effects of water scarcity through inventions of methods among them including water treatment. Water treatment is the term that referrs to purification of water for domestic or industrial use. Generally it involves production of clean water through the following various methods drinking water treatment, wastewater treatment and industrial water treatment. This website makes it easy for parties to be able to access water treatment facilitieds particularly around their counties. Then through access they can help with treatment or create more facilities. If you are looking towards making every drop count then you are at the right place.


Dispose of waste water from properties not connected to mains drainage

What

A biodigester is a treatment plant that safely treats and disposes of wastewater from properties without access to black and grey water drainage.

How

The tank uses natural micro-organisms and oxygen to break down solids in the wastewater. Biodigesters in our range produce a clear odourless effluent for safe disposal.

Why

Biodigesters are available in a wide range of sizes for homes and businesses and are self-contained. No chemicals are needed and some do not need to be emptied at all, so nasty work is avoided.
